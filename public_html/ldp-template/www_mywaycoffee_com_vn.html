ABOUT US

My Way Coffee is a Vietnamese coffee brand with core range products based on Robusta beans - top quality coffee bean in the world with strong flavors and distinctive charactersistics

My Way was established since 2006 by professionals and experts in F& B industry and coffee culture. With the cooperation of Vietnamese & international investors, we aim to bring Vietnamese coffee to the world market. Our new style coffee products will rapidly connect coffee lovers across the globe

We are currently growing our coffee on the fertile soil of Daklak and Highlands region of Vietnam. Our plantation covers approximately 5,000 hectares of Robusta and Arabica
